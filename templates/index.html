New Investment Scam Leverages AI

A sophisticated investment scam called "Nomani" is using AI-generated deepfake videos of celebrities and public figures to promote fraudulent investment opportunities on social media. Scammers create fake ads and profiles, often targeting people who have previously been scammed, promising to help them recover their lost money.

Deepfakes to social media

With the rise in online shopping, scammers are increasingly using social media platforms to promote fake online stores and fraudulent ads. These ads often feature heavily discounted products or celebrity endorsements, luring unsuspecting shoppers.

Social media scams

Romance scams continue to be a significant issue on social media platforms. Scammers create fake profiles on dating apps and social media, often using stolen photos and fabricated identities. They build relationships with their victims, sometimes over months or even years, before manipulating them into sending money.
